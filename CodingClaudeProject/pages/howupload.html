How to Upload

Share your study materials with UTHM students in 3 simple steps

1

Prepare your file

Make sure your file is ready and contains study material relevant to your UTHM subject. Remove any personal information such as your student ID or name from the document before uploading.

PDF DOCX PPTX JPG / PNG Max 20 MB
2

Fill in the details

Provide accurate metadata so other students can easily find your material. Select your faculty, enter the correct subject code, and write a short description of what the file covers.

Faculty Subject code Description
3

Submit for review

Your file will be reviewed by the Studycademia team before it goes live. This usually takes 24–48 hours. You will receive a notification once your material is published and available to other students.

Reviewed within 48 hours

Upload guidelines

  • Academic content only — lecture notes, summaries, past papers
  • Remove personal identifiers from your document
  • Do not upload copyrighted textbooks or exam answer keys
  • Do not upload materials from other platforms without permission
